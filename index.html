Proof you can verify. A photo's pixels, time, location and device, cryptographically sealed on the capturing phone.

Verify a photo →
  1. 1Open the sealed photo's original file (not a chat-app copy — those re-compress it and break the seal).
  2. 2Drag it into the verify page. It checks in your browser — nothing is uploaded.
  3. 3It recomputes the pixel hash and checks the hardware signature. One changed pixel or field → fails.
